# Canton Development Fund Allocation Model

## Overview
The Development Fund is financed through a programmatic allocation of 5% of future Canton Coin emissions.

## Mechanism
Rather than relying on a premine or centralized treasury, the allocation is embedded into the network emission model.

## Governance Oversight
Funding allocation and milestone approvals are governed under the CIP-0100 framework.

## Sustainability
Because funding derives from future emissions, the model is durable and predictable, scaling alongside network growth.
