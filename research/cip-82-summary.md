# CIP-0082 Summary

## Objective
Introduce a programmatic allocation of 5% of future Canton Coin emissions to establish a sustainable Development Fund.

## Governance Change Introduced
Defines the structural allocation mechanism and integrates fund distribution into network governance rather than relying on a premine or treasury.

## Funding Mechanism Detail
5% of future network emissions are programmatically redirected to the Development Fund under governance oversight.

## Validator Voting Process
Approved through the established Canton governance framework by the Voting Group and Super Validators.

## Expected Ecosystem Impact
Creates a predictable, long-term funding source to support protocol improvements, tooling, infrastructure, and ecosystem growth.
