# CIP-0100 Summary

## Objective
Define the governance and review framework for administering the Development Fund.

## Governance Change Introduced
Establishes the Tech & Ops Committee, Voting Group approval process, and milestone-based funding release structure.

## Funding Mechanism Detail
Specifies evaluation, approval, milestone tracking, and payment release procedures.

## Validator Voting Process
Funding decisions are reviewed by Tech & Ops and approved by the Voting Group.

## Expected Ecosystem Impact
Introduces structured oversight, accountability, and transparent operational governance for fund distribution.
